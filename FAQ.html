Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question: How much life insurance coverage do I need?


Answer: The amount of coverage you need depends on various factors such as your financial obligation, income, and future needs. A financial advisor can help assess your situation and recommend an appropriate coverage amount.

Question: What types of life insurance policie are offered?


Sun Life Insurance may offer various types of policies, including term life, whole life, and universal life insurance. Each type has its features, benefits, and costs.

Question: Can I change my life insurance coverage over time?


Yes, many life insurance policies offer flexibility. You may be able to adjust your coverage, change beneficiaries, or convert a term policy to a permanent one, depending on the terms of your policy.

Question: How do premiums work, and are they guaranteed?


Answer: Premiums are the payments you make for your life insurance policy. The structure and guarantee of premiums depend on the type of policy. Some policies have fixed premiums, while others may be subject to change.
