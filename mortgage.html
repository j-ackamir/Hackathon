Real estate can serve as a hedge against inflation, with property values potentially appreciating. Economic instability from inflation may influence mortgage qualification as lenders adopt more cautious practices. Staying informed about market conditions is essential for homeowners and prospective buyers in navigating inflation-related challenges.
